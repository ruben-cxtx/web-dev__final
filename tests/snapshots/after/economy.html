Online Banking & Fintech

Milestones

  • 2000

    First local online banking launches

    Banco General states it was the first Panamanian bank to offer online banking (circa 2000).

  • 2019–2025

    Bank-led wallets & instant transfers expand

    Yappy (Banco General) becomes the leading P2P and merchant wallet; ACH Xpress broadens instant bank transfers.

  • 2024

    Nequi exits Panama

    Nequi ceases operations in Panama on Dec 31, 2024; users migrate to Banistmo channels.

Future Trends

  • Online education (MOOCs) & higher-ed e-learning

    UTP & UP offer e-learning; MOOCs accessible via global platforms.

  • Telemedicine & digital health

    Telehealth legal framework (2021) and MINSA program expansion in 2024–2025.

    Ongoing digitization, predictive analytics use cases around the Canal and logistics ecosystem.

    Rising wallet use and contactless growth; digital payment rails via Telered/ACH.
